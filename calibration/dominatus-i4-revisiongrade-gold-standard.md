## 🔴 MUST FIX — Structural + Authority Failures

### 1. POV Instability / Entity Bleed
**Criterion:** POV & Voice Control (Wave 5)

**Problem:** Hyla POV is repeatedly broken by omniscient / Realm / abstract intrusion without clear boundary markers.

**Original:**
> "Their pathetic pleas clung to the stone, soaking deep into its marrow."
> "Countless generations of ineptitude now her burden."

**Issue:**
- "pathetic" = authorial judgment (not Hyla-filtered)
- "ineptitude" = external evaluation, not embodied perception

**Revised Direction:** Filter all judgment through Hyla's perception OR isolate as Realm voice.

---

### 2. Scene Entry Violation
**Criterion:** Narrative Drive & Momentum (Vol II Doctrine: Scene Entry Discipline)

**Problem:** Environmental opening without action → momentum drag.

**Original:**
> "Earth scrubbed Aqua World, too—one poisoned rain at a time. The surface writhed with putrescence…"

**Issue:**
- Atmospheric-first opening
- No character action anchor
- Violates Narrative Drive & Momentum

**Revised Direction:** Begin with Hyla action / perception / decision, THEN environment.

---

### 3. Thematic Over-Statement
**Criterion:** Thematic Integration (Wave 21, Wave 54)

**Problem:** Theme is stated directly instead of dramatized.

**Original:**
> "This was no sickness. It was a war declaration."

**Issue:**
- On-the-nose thematic labeling
- Removes reader inference

**Revised Direction:** Convert to observable threat / consequence / behavior.

---

### 4. Metaphor Overload + Chain Conflict
**Criterion:** Prose Control (Wave 48, Wave 58)

**Problem:** Too many simultaneous metaphor systems → dilution of authority.

**Original:**
> "Jagged rocks clawed at the sky like broken teeth."
> "colors lay buried beneath black muck, as if mourning themselves."

**Issue:**
- Claw + teeth + mourning = competing metaphor fields
- No single dominant image

**Revised Direction:** Select ONE governing metaphor per paragraph.

---

### 5. Personification Density Violation
**Criterion:** Prose Control (Wave 47)

**Problem:** Environment is over-animated without consequence linkage.

**Original:**
> "The surface writhed…"
> "colors lay buried…"
> "water answered"

**Issue:**
- Excess personification reduces impact
- Becomes stylistic noise instead of signal

**Revised Direction:** Keep only personification tied to Realm agency or plot consequence.

---

### 6. Redundant Scene Function
**Criterion:** Scene Construction & Function

**Problem:** Council debate repeats same argument cycle without escalation.

**Pattern:** Interbreeding debate loops: purity → survival → risk → belief (repeating)

**Issue:**
- Scene redundancy (Failure Map Zone 4)
- No escalation ladder progression

**Revised Direction:** Each speaker must:
- Introduce NEW risk, OR
- Escalate consequence, OR
- Shift decision state

---

### 7. Escalation Gap Before Attack
**Criterion:** Narrative Drive & Momentum

**Problem:** Fish attack arrives without sufficient escalation ramp.

**Original transition:** Debate → sudden breach

**Issue:**
- Feels injected, not inevitable
- Weakens causality chain

**Revised Direction:** Seed: barrier weakness, fish pressure, prior near-breach signals.

### 8. Internal Thought Boundary Confusion
**Criterion:** POV & Voice Control (Wave 16)

**Original:**

**Issue:** Good concept, but surrounding text not clearly anchored in thought mode.

**Revised Direction:** Standardize: italics OR consistent free indirect style.

## Key Diagnostic Distinctions (from iterative refinement)

These principles emerged from the full evaluation dialogue and represent the governing intelligence of the critique:

1. **Density ≠ Excess.** The chapter's power is in invasive pressure, spiritual oppression, and cosmological dominance. General AI reads this as "too much" and trims. RevisionGrade distinguishes the manuscript's pressure system from true redundancy.

2. **Authority transfer must be evaluated before line-level critique.** Structure first → escalation and scene function → authority control → line-level compression. This sequencing prevents "improving the wrong thing first."

3. **Multiplicity is not the problem; boundary clarity is.** Multiple consciousness layers (Hyla, Realm, Gorf, Spores) are the manuscript's operating logic. The issue is unclear handoff between them, not their existence.

4. **Pressure can be restored without restoring density.** The rewrite path is: compression without flattening, clarity without conventionalizing, control without de-mythologizing.

5. **The unaided AI rewrite is the best evidence against the system that produced it.** It is competent but moves the chapter toward a more standard, less dangerous novel. RevisionGrade provides terms for what is being lost.
