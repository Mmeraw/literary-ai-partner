## Part 7 — What the Pipeline Must Learn

The fit-gap between the offending evaluation and the truth target reveals the following systemic failures that RevisionGrade's pipeline must be trained to avoid:

### Failure Mode 1: Canon Blindness to Secondary POV Layers
**What happened:** The pipeline analyzed the primary human lane (Brutus/Chey) and the primary frog governance lane (Hyla/Zimeon/Rana as Dead Zone traveler) — and stopped. It did not recognize the newt/salamander counter-world (Newton, Lacerta, Twillow, Twen) as a structural spine.
**Root cause hypothesis:** Chunk-map/reduce pass likely allocated less token weight to newt/salamander scenes because they appear in fewer chapters and shorter passages than the dominant Brutus or Aqua World scenes. The synthesis pass then underweighted or omitted what the chunk pass had deprioritized.
**Gate to add:** Any evaluation of this manuscript that does not name Newton, Lacerta, Twillow, and Twen must be rejected at the acceptance check layer.

### Failure Mode 2: Relationship Spine Omission
**What happened:** The Rana–Newton rescue/healing/friendship arc — which the truth target calls the "moral hinge of the excerpt" — is entirely absent from the offending evaluation. Rana is analyzed only as a solo Dead Zone explorer.
**Root cause hypothesis:** Cross-species scenes may be harder to track in chunk reduce because they involve two characters from different world layers appearing together. The reduce pass may have filed Newton's scenes under "newt world" and Rana's scenes under "frog world" without recognizing the Rana–Newton scenes as a merged arc.
**Gate to add:** Any evaluation of this manuscript that does not identify Rana–Newton as a relationship spine must be rejected.

### Failure Mode 3: Object/Medicine System Blindness
**What happened:** The juniper berry medicine system, witch hazel, wild lettuce, parotoid slime, and fungus/blight — collectively the "Newtlore medicine system" — are entirely absent. The offending evaluation has no medicine section, no medicine recommendations, and no recognition that this system exists.
**Root cause hypothesis:** Medicine/ecological details in short passages may have been classified as descriptive texture rather than plot systems during chunk reduce. Without a specific medicine-system extractor in the pass, these details were dropped.
**Gate to add:** Any evaluation of this manuscript that does not name the juniper berry medicine system must be rejected.

### Failure Mode 4: Unsupported Vocabulary Generation
**What happened:** The pipeline used "poaching" and "poacher" 8 times. This language is not consistently supported by the source text and frames the human ecological threat inaccurately.
**Root cause hypothesis:** "Poaching" is a high-frequency training association for "humans taking animals from natural habitats." The model defaulted to this label rather than extracting the text's own vocabulary (frog capture, chemical contamination, environmental degradation).
**Gate to add:** Source-grounding audit must flag any use of "poaching" that does not correspond to source text. Correct vocabulary must be enforced in prompt engineering.

### Failure Mode 5: Closure Score Deflation Due to Arc Blindness
**What happened:** Narrative Closure scored 4.0 in the offending evaluation — the lowest criterion score. The truth target scores it 6.0. The +2.0 delta is the largest single-criterion gap.
**Root cause hypothesis:** When the Rana–Newton arc is absent, the excerpt appears to end without any emotional payoff. In reality, Newton's rescue, Lacerta's death, Rana's healing of Newton, and Newton's juniper medicine discovery are all local arc completions. The model couldn't score what it hadn't seen.
**Gate to add:** Closure scoring must be validated against the relationship spine ledger. If major relationship arcs are unscored, the closure score is invalid.

## Part 8 — Training / Gate Specification

The following acceptance checks must be added to the RevisionGrade evaluation pipeline for manuscripts containing multi-species amphibian worlds, counter-world characters, or cross-species healing arcs:

**REQUIRED DETECTION (evaluation fails if absent):**
1. All named supporting characters in the uploaded manuscript must appear in the character analysis — not just the primary POV lane
2. Cross-species relationship arcs must be explicitly identified as relationship spines
3. Any object/medicine system with named components (juniper berry, medicine, slime, etc.) must generate a named entry in the object ledger
4. Source vocabulary must be extracted from the text; high-frequency training associations ("poaching") must be checked against source text before being used as evaluation labels
5. Closure scoring must be validated against the relationship spine ledger — closure cannot be scored low solely because a relationship arc was missed

**FAILURE CONDITIONS (evaluation is rejected at gate if any of these occur):**
- Offending eval omits Newton, Lacerta, Twillow, or Twen from a manuscript where they are present
- Offending eval omits Rana–Newton from the relationship spine ledger
- Offending eval omits the juniper berry / medicine system from the object ledger
- Offending eval uses "poaching" as primary ecological threat label without source grounding
- Offending eval scores Narrative Closure below 5.0 while relationship arcs remain unanalyzed
