# WAVE-55-L: Selective Authority Compression (Literary Horror Mode)

**Canon ID:** WAVE-55-L  
**Status:** FROZEN  
**Type:** Execution Canon (Wave Variant)  
**Authority:** Mike Meraw  
**Frozen Date:** 2026-05-28  
**Destination:** Volume I - Wave Canon (Tsunami 5 / Literary Authority), cross-ref Volume II (PROSE, TONE), Volume II-A, Volume III, Volume IV

## Purpose

Constrain Wave 55 so that for density-based, atmosphere-driven literary horror, compression removes only authority-weakening excess, not texture, mythic layering, or ritual cadence.

## Scope

Triggered when:
- Manuscript is tagged `mode = "literary_dense_horror"` or equivalent
- Volume I Wave 55 ("Authority Kill Switch") is active

Applies to:
- Line-level and paragraph-level compression decisions after Waves 31 and 33-40 are satisfied

## Core Doctrine

1. **Wave 55-L is a scalpel with a governor, not a chainsaw.** If a pass removes texture, ritual chains, or mythic tone, it is misapplied and must be rejected.
2. **Structure before compression.** Wave 55-L may only run after Wave 31 pressure continuity is fixed and Waves 33-40 escalation/pacing spine are in place.
3. **Compression targets "echo," not "engine."** Remove flat echoes that restate what the scene already made the reader feel. Preserve lines that carry pressure, texture, or ritual function.

## Protected Elements (55-L-P)

These elements are presumptively protected from compression:
- **Sensory layering and environmental presence:** Smell, sound, tactile atmosphere that create ecosystem dread or immersion
- **Ritual fragment chains and cadence:** Repeated word/phrase patterns used to build rhythm or liturgical tone
- **Symbolic and mythic objects in active use:** Toadstone/shard, owl motifs, recurring images that carry thematic weight
- **Anchor lines in 15/10 passages:** Lines designated as ANCHOR once a chapter hits final calibrated voice

If a candidate line falls into any protected category, Wave 55-L must treat it as non-removable unless explicitly re-anchored by the author.

## Valid Compression Targets (55-L-T)

Wave 55-L may target:
- **Post-impact reinforcement lines:** Explanatory or echo sentences that follow a killer line and only restate its meaning
- **Dialogue explanatory stacking:** Lines that explain what is already evident from dialogue and behavior
- **Flat intensity stacking:** Multiple sentences at the same intensity level that do not escalate or transform
- **Bridge phrases that merely narrate transitions:** When the following image already shows the shift
- **Minor physiological filler in clusters:** Extra nods, swallows, exhalations that create Gate 15.1-risk clustering near strong beats

## Forbidden Compression Actions (55-L-F)

Wave 55-L must NOT:
- Remove lines that add a new image, sensation, or pressure vector, even if they sound redundant
- Cut the first instance of an important emotional or symbolic beat; only weaker later echoes are eligible
- Break a functional ritual chain purely to tighten word count

## Pass/Fail Rule

A revision pass is non-compliant with WAVE-55-L if it:
- Removes protected texture/ritual/myth lines
- Cuts authority-building lines instead of authority-weakening echoes
- Produces prose that is tighter but less atmospheric, less haunted, or less authoritative

Compliant only when: only authority-weakening echoes were cut, and no protected elements were removed.
