## Section I -- Wave Execution Law

### WAVE-31-LW: Chapter-Ending Pressure Doctrine
**Title:** Pressure Must Remain in Motion
**Doctrine:** Endings must not land in understanding -- they must end in consequence already in motion.
**Law:**
- Pressure seeds (unseen disturbance, ongoing threat, unresolved consequence) are the correct closing move
- Prohibited: generic action beats, soft landings, thematic resolution
- Execution order: Wave 31 (carry pressure) before Waves 33-40 (escalation shape) before Wave 55-L (compression)
**System Mapping:** chapter-ending-analyzer.ts, wave planner ending pressure gates

### WAVE-55-L: Selective Authority Compression Doctrine
**Title:** Compression Must Not Kill the Spell
**Doctrine:** Cut reinforcement, not ritual. Remove flat echo, not escalating cadence.
**Law:**
- Wave 55 is a scalpel with a governor, not a chainsaw
- KEEP: sensory layering, environmental presence, ritual fragment chains, symbolic repetition
- CUT: reinforcement lines, flat intensity stacking, bridge phrases that explain what prose already conveys
- Distinguish ritual repetition (escalating/re-angled) from comfort repeat (flat echo)
**System Mapping:** diff-intelligence.ts, ritual-protection.ts, wave safety matrix

## Section II -- Ritual and Voice Law

### RITUAL-EDITOR-1: Ritual Arbitration Doctrine
**Title:** Repetition Must Evolve or Die
**Doctrine:** If a line adds image, escalation, or psychological shift, it is preserved. If it merely repeats, it is cut.
**Law:**
- KEEP if: new image, escalation in intensity, shift in control/psychological state
- CUT if: same meaning, weaker repeat, comfort-based echo
**System Mapping:** REC-1A logic, escalation-analyzer.ts
