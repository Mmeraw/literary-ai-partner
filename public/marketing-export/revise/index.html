The contract

Every opportunity carries its own reasoning chain.

No commentary. No vague advice. Each RevisionOpportunity is a machine-governable work order with six required elements.

  1. 01

    Evidence

    Anchored text with character offsets — the exact span being addressed.

  2. 02

    Symptom

    The observable problem in that text. What a reader would feel.

  3. 03

    Cause

    The mechanism producing the symptom. Why it happens, not just that it does.

  4. 04

    Fix direction

    A bounded revision move. Specific enough to act on without further interpretation.

  5. 05

    Reader effect

    What measurably improves if the fix is applied.

  6. 06

    Mistake-proofing

    What must be preserved or avoided so the fix introduces no new damage.

Long-form mode

At 25,000 words, the architecture becomes mandatory.

An 84,000-word manuscript is not a stretched chapter. Above the threshold, Revise switches to operating-system mode: WAVE intelligence and the Golden Spine become contract-enforced surfaces, not optional metadata.

  • Continuity chain. Promises opened, promises closed, threads still live at the midpoint.
  • Pressure continuity. Where narrative tension plateaus across chapter boundaries.
  • Pacing valleys. Scene density drops mapped to specific chapter ranges.
  • Unresolved promises. Setups that have not yet paid off, with evidence anchors.
  • Thematic propagation. How the manuscript’s motifs travel — or don’t — through the second act.

§ If wordCount ≥ 25000 and the wave surface is missing, the contract fails closed.

RevisionOpportunity

The first-class primitive.

Recommendations are commentary. Opportunities are work orders — bounded scope, diagnosis, executable pathway, handoff semantics, machine governance. 

// EvaluationResultV2 — the seam between Evaluate, Revise, and Trustpath
type RevisionOpportunity = {
  id: string;
  criterionKey: string;
  title: string;
  severity: 'must' | 'should' | 'could';
  leverage: 'spine-critical' | 'high' | 'medium' | 'local';
  confidenceRisk: 'low' | 'moderate' | 'high';
  evidence: { snippet: string; charStart?: number; charEnd?: number };
  symptom: string;
  cause: string;
  fixDirection: string;
  readerEffect: string;
  mistakeProofing: string;
  revisionOptions?: RevisionOption[]; // up to three
  handoffTarget: 'revise' | 'trustpath-review' | 'trustpath-safe';
  state: OpportunityState; // pending → reviewing → accepted | customized | rejected | deferred
};

Fail-closed on missing fields

QG_REVISION_OPPORTUNITY_REQUIRED_FIELDS rejects any opportunity that cannot stand on its own as a work order.

No semantic placeholders

QG_REVISION_OPPORTUNITY_NON_DEGENERATE rejects structurally complete but semantically empty payloads. Generic causes don’t pass.

Deterministic queue

QG_REVISE_QUEUE_DETERMINISM guarantees that identical input always yields identical ordering.

Lossless handoff

QG_REVISE_HANDOFF_ID_FIDELITY ensures every link from Evaluate lands on the exact same opportunity in Revise.
